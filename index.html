What we do

I do whatever it takes to help brands and organizations serve their communities online. With 20+ years of experience and a healthy skepticism of digital trends, I stay focused on the experience your customers have and the digital channels they use to connect with you.

I founded Skeptical Digital with a mission to share my unique perspective with like-minded organizations – with strongly held values and a desire to improve people’s lives. It doesn’t matter whether you’re a scrappy small business or a multinational institution: if you’re looking to better use your digital channels to make meaningful connections and build your community, I’m here to help.
